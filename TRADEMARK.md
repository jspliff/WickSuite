# Wick — Trademark & Brand Usage Policy

The **Wick** name, **Wick's** possessive prefix, the **Wick logomark** (teardrop flame flanked by fel-green L-brackets), the **Wick wordmark** (Cinzel 900), and the composite visual system (locked palette + L-bracket chrome + naming convention) are **trademarks of Wick**.

The MIT license on the code does **not** grant you any rights to these brand assets. They are protected separately.

## What you MAY do

- **Reference the project by name** in a truthful, descriptive way — "I use Wick's CD Tracker", "inspired by the Wick suite", "this is a fork of Wick's Trade Hall".
- **Link to the repos or CurseForge pages** using the official name.
- **Fork the code** under the MIT terms and make your own addon — but you must **rename it** (see below).
- **Cite the brand identity** in design writeups or articles, with attribution.

## What you MAY NOT do

- **Do not publish a fork, derivative, or competing addon using the Wick name, the "Wick's [X]" naming pattern, or the Wick logomark.** Rename your fork to something clearly distinct (example: if you fork Wick's CD Tracker, call it "YourName CD Tracker", not "Wick's CD Tracker — Remix").
- **Do not use the Wick logomark, wordmark, or L-bracket chrome system** as identifying marks of your own addon, project, service, guild, or commercial offering.
- **Do not publish "Wick-compatible" or "official Wick"** labels on addons not authored by Wick.
- **Do not combine the Wick brand with adult, hateful, or deceptive content.**

## Renaming a fork

If you fork any Wick addon and republish it:

1. Change the `.toc` `## Title:` field — remove "Wick's".
2. Change the `## Author:` field to your own name.
3. Replace `logo.svg` with your own mark, or remove it.
4. Remove the L-bracket fel-green chrome OR change the accent color substantially (you're welcome to use L-brackets as a generic UI pattern — the protected thing is the specific `#4FC778` fel-green + `#0D0A14` void combination applied as a system).
5. Note in your README that the code was forked from the Wick suite (MIT attribution).
