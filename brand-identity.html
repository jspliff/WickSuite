06 — Naming Convention
Wick's
+
[Function]
+
[Noun]
Possessive prefix
Always "Wick's" — not "Wick" alone. Possessive signals authorship and creates a consistent family across the suite.
Functional noun
Two words max. Clear, literal, no fantasy puns. Raider and casual alike must immediately understand the addon's purpose.
Tone
Professional. No abbreviations in the full name (CD is fine in-game shorthand). No numerals. No v2 suffixes in the brand name.
