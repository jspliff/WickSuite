This is the integrated wordmark — the flame-and-candle logo substitutes for the "i" in Wick's. Use it for headers, email signatures, Discord profiles, or anywhere a textual brand fits better than the square logo. Pair the stacked lockups (Wick's over [ADDON NAME]) as per-addon variants.

The SVG uses CSS custom properties (--accent, --letter, --base) so recoloring is just a style tweak.
